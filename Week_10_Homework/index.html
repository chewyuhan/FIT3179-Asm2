Data Visualizations: Global Terrorism Insights (2012-2022)

Geographical Distribution on Global Terrorist Index

In the turbulent narrative of global terrorism spanning the years 2012 to 2022, our interactive map unveils a chilling tale.

Afghanistan, Iraq, and Syria emerge as perennial protagonists in this saga of unrest, their consistent high scores painting a vivid picture of enduring challenges.

Users can play with the Year Slider to control the year that shows an interesting trend regarding the Global Terrorist Score.

Global Terrorist Attack Per Country

The data on terrorist attacks highlights a persistent threat in Asia, particularly in countries like Afghanistan, Iraq, Nigeria, Pakistan, Somalia, and Syria. Fluctuations in attack frequency indicate evolving geopolitical dynamics. Iraq, Afghanistan, Nigeria, and Syria consistently face high fatalities and injuries, emphasizing the severe impact of terrorism. Hostage situations, notably in Afghanistan, Iraq, and Syria, add complexity to security challenges. Emerging hotspots like Mali, Burkina Faso, and Mozambique signify the evolving global terrorism landscape. Despite international efforts, sustained collaboration is crucial to address root causes and effectively combat terrorism globally.
